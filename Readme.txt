It's the planet earth where I've shown the village, city and hill areas in different sections. (Drawing motto is Save Planet)

1. The earth is rotating along z-axis. 
	a) Press "p" to rotate along y-axis.
	b) Press "q" to rotate along x-axis.
	c)Left click on mouse to stop the rotation and right click on mouse to start rotation.

2. Press "f" - front view
   Press "l" - left view
   Press "r" - right view
   Press "b" - back view
   Press "t" - top view
   Press "m" - bottom view
   
3. Enter inside the planet by pressing "Up" and "Bottom" arrow on keyboard. 
   "Save Planet" box is rotating inside.
